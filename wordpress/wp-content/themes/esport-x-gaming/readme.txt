Start your new gaming website, forum, or eCommerce shop with Esport X Gaming. If you want to create a blog, review, or news site for gamers then we got you covered with every feature you need for that. It comes with Elementor website builder, bbPress forum, and BuddyPress integration. You and your users can post videos, images, live streams, and much more. The forum integrations are important for those of you who want to build a clan, team, or guild website. Use the integrated plugins to create polls, discussions, and forms. Comes with multi-purpose features and unlimited color schemes for your website. The theme is suitable for game developers as well. It's easy to set up and has a minimal look and feel that you can customize to your liking. Sustainability is deeply integrated into the theme, Esport X Gaming is a low-impact WordPress theme.
